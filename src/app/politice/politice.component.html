Privacy Policy

We understand the importance of the privacy. Following describes what types of information personal information we receive and collect when you use Images PyXpert, and how the we protect. We hope this privacy policy helps you make informed decisions about the use of our application.

Data Access Disclosure

Images PyXpert uses Google login to authenticate users. When doing so, Google may collect certain login information, such as your username and associated email address. This information is used solely to authenticate the user and is not stored or used by Images PyXpert for any other purpose. By using our application, you agree that Google may collect and process this information in accordance with its own privacy policies.

Information on Data Protection

Images PyXpert is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users' data. We do not store or retain any personal information of users in our databases. Google login information and metadata of images selected for processing are used temporarily during the user session and are not stored or shared with third parties. Additionally, all communications between the app and Google servers are done securely using standard encryption protocols. If you have any questions or concerns about data protection at Images PyXpert, please do not hesitate to get in touch.

Information we collect

Images PyXpert NOT collects the information you provide to us when you use our application. This information is only visible and available to the user, this may include:

  • Google login information, including username and the email.
  • Information about photos selected for processing, such as image metadata.

Use of collected information

The application uses the captured information WITHOUT saving these data, for the following purposes:

  • Facilitate user authentication through login Google.
  • Process selected photos according to user preferences.
  • Improve and personalize the user experience in Images PyXpert.
